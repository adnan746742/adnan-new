Legacy & Leadership

Our force of success

Founding father and the man who is one of the members of the group who set up the first industry of Kota Stone (Limestone). The roots of the Adnan Group trace back to the 20th century in the picturesque town of Suket, nestled amidst the Rajasthan southeast region.

OUR STORY

Sulaiman Baig

Mr. Sulaiman Baig began his journey as a rickshaw wala in the 1950s, later shifting to limestone mining with handmade tools. His foresight and hard work laid the foundation for the limestone industry, inspiring future generations with his pioneering spirit and transforming the sector into a thriving industry.

Mannan Ahmed

Mr. Mannan Ahmed, the younger son of Sulaiman Baig, carried on his father’s legacy by expanding the limestone business. He introduced a trading system for Limestone and founded Meena Stone, a venture focused on diamond tools, taking the family business to greater heights and into new industries.

Adnan Ahmed

Mr. Adnan joined the family business after completing his Civil Services journey, bringing a fresh perspective and innovative ideas. He played a key role in boosting the Adnan Group’s growth by launching a services-based company, marking a new phase of expansion and development for the organization.

Azmam Ahmed

Mr. Azmam Ahmed, the youngest member of this generation, introduced new trading ideas to the Adnan Group. Leveraging his experience, he efficiently managed the production department, playing a key role in enhancing the group’s operations and driving growth.
